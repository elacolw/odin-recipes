Lasagna

Making lasagnas can be time consuming but with this recipe you'll become the star in the kitchen.

Follow this step-by-step recipe to make the best lasagna of all-times!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna's Pasta
  • Ragu
  • Besciamella
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Take the pasta out of the fridge and put it in the plate
  2. Put some ragu and mozzarella on top on the first layer of pasta
  3. Procede until the termination of all the ingredients
