Pasta Aglio & Olio

No two aglio e olio recipes are alike, but this one is the classic method.

The key is slowly toasting the garlic slices to a perfect golden brown in the olive oil. If it's too light, you don't get the full flavor and if it's too dark it gets bitter.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Aglio
  • Olio
  • Peperoncino

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta and the garlic in two separate pans
  2. Add peperoncino and olive oil with the garlic
  3. When the pasta is ready, assemble everything
