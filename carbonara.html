Pasta Carbonara

Carbonara is a Roman dish made with eggs, hard cheese, and cured pork. Its signature rich and silky sauce comes from beaten eggs tossed with hot pasta.

The trick to making carbonara is making sure the pasta is hot enough to cook the eggs, but not so hot that they curdle.

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino
  • eggs
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. cook the pasta and the guanciale in two separate pans
  2. use one egg yolk per person to make the sauce
  3. put pecorino with egg yolks and create the cream
  4. if necessary add some pasta water to make everything smooth
  5. add some pepper
  6. when everything is cooked assemble the dish
